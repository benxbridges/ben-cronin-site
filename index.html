About

I'm one half of Gilligan Moss, a producer and songwriter, and a person who can't stop building things on the internet.

My work spans electronic music, production for other artists, generative audio experiments, and the occasional website built from scratch. I also used to write a music newsletter called Two Songs.

I like the places where music, technology, and design overlap — and I try to make things that live there.
